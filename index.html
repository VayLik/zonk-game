Rules of Zonk

The goal is to reach the target score before your opponent does.

  • Roll all 6 dice. Set aside at least one scoring die or combination.
  • If you set aside all 6 dice, you get "Hot Dice" and earn a free reroll.
  • If a roll yields no scoring dice, you "Zonk" and lose turn points.
  • Hit "Bank Score" to end your turn safely.

How to Score

The Golden One: Single 1 = 100 pts.
The Handy Five: Single 5 = 50 pts.
The Jackpot: Three 1s = 1000 pts.
Three of a Kind: Face value x 100.
The Straight: 1 to 6 = 1500 pts.
Three Pairs: e.g., 2-2, 4-4, 6-6 = 1500 pts.
